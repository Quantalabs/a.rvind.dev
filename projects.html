Projects

I'm currently a member of Red Tie Robotics (FRC #1458), and have thus developed numerous FRC-focused projects over the past 2 seasons in FRC. Namely, frustrations regarding the default dashboard's and their layouts prompted me to create my own dashboard using Svelte, called Lemonade. I plan on extending this to include integration with custom button board for robot control, as well as modularizing the dashboard to better fit other teams' needs.

In my freshman year at the Ragin' C-Biscuits (FRC #1280), I developed the infrastructure for storing, viewing, and managing scouting data from ScoutingPASS.

For the past year, I've been a member of Hack Club, and have developed some projects for various events. As a result, I worked on my first hardware project, a simple macropad called Basmati.

I also created ElectionBook, a web app that analyzed the 2024 U.S. presidential election using google search and news data in the months, weeks, and days preceding the election.

I also created epispot, which is a package for modeling infectious diseases in TS/JS.

Finally, I create various small utilities and projects for personal use, such as:

  • Diet, which helps me update my GitHub profile with my favorite research papers.
  • .files, which contains my Arch Linux system configuration (I use arch, btw).
  • a.rvind.dev, this website.

For new projects, I'm particularly interested in finding ideas that mix my passions of music and math, as well as more applying my programming skills to other disciplines, like physics or chemistry. I'm also trying to develop more complex PCBs and hardware projects, such as my own guitar pedal.
